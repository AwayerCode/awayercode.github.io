## What to Expect

This blog will cover topics such as:

- **Technology** - Programming tutorials, tool reviews, and industry insights

- **Personal** - Thoughts on productivity, learning, and growth

## Features

This blog includes several modern features:

### Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark themes using the moon/sun button in the header.

### Search

Use the [search page](/search/) to find posts quickly across the entire blog.

### RSS Feed

Subscribe to the [RSS feed](/feed.xml) to stay updated with new posts.

### Responsive Design

The site works great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
